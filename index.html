I'm Hiruni Weerawansha — an entrepreneur, career coach, and content creator based in Colombo, Sri Lanka. As Co-Founder of DocxDeveloper Pvt. Ltd, I lead a technology-supportive company delivering document solutions and tech support services to businesses across Sri Lanka and beyond.

My philosophy is rooted in one belief: clarity of purpose is the most powerful business tool. Whether launching a venture, mentoring someone through a career transition, or crafting content that elevates a brand — I bring strategy, precision, and genuine care to everything I do.

Alongside my corporate leadership, I independently run DocxDesigner — a specialist CV and document design service — and Hiru's Studio, a curated fashion store. Each venture is an expression of a different dimension of my entrepreneurial identity.
